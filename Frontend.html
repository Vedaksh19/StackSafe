The Future of Digital Inheritance

StackSafe combines cutting-edge technologies to provide a trustless, secure, and autonomous solution.

Decentralized Vaults

Deploy your own autonomous smart contract on the Stacks blockchain. You are the sole owner; we never touch your keys or assets.

Biometric Verification

Your nominee's identity is secured with a two-factor process: a one-time password and advanced facial recognition, preventing unauthorized access.

Bitcoin-Grade Security

Built on Stacks, every transaction and vault operation is ultimately settled and secured by the most robust blockchain in the world: Bitcoin.

Simple, Secure, Automated

Follow a straightforward process to protect your assets for the future.

1

Deploy Vault

Connect your wallet, define your nominee, set a "heartbeat" interval, and deploy your personal smart contract.

2

Ping to Stay Active

Periodically send a simple "ping" transaction to your vault. This proves you're active and resets the inheritance timer.

3

Automated Claim

If the timer expires, your nominee can initiate a claim. After passing OTP and face verification, the assets are transferred automatically.

Engineered for Trust

Our hybrid security model provides defense-in-depth for your digital legacy.

On-Chain Assurance

  • ✓**Trustless Smart Contracts:** Code is law. The vault operates exactly as programmed on the blockchain, without any central party.
  • ✓**Clarity Language:** Built with a decidable language that prevents common exploits like re-entrancy, ensuring predictable behavior.
  • ✓**Bitcoin Finality:** All vault actions are anchored to the Bitcoin network, providing the highest level of security and immutability.

Off-Chain Verification

  • ✓**Robust 2FA:** The OTP + Face Recognition flow ensures that only the intended nominee can ever initiate a claim.
  • ✓**No Image Storage:** We never store your nominee's photos. Only a secure, mathematical representation (FaceID) is kept for verification.
  • ✓**Encrypted Secrets:** All sensitive off-chain data, like the encrypted password, is protected using industry-standard AES encryption.
